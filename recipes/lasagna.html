Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat - Use sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic - An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with meat to add flavour
  • Tomato products - Can of crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and paste
  • Spices and seasonings - Use fresh parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Cheeses - Parmesan, mozzerella, ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
